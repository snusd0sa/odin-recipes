Chicken

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken legs with skin
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 2 tsp paprika powder
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp dried selleri
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
How to cook:
Airfryer: Preheat to 200c
Mix all spices and oil in a bowl
Dry the chicken legs with some paper
Massage em with the spicemix
Put in airfryer and cook for total 20-25min. Turn em around about halftime.
